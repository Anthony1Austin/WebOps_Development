# Special Event Designs Website Template

A modern, elegant website template for event planning and floral design businesses. Perfect for wedding planners, corporate event coordinators, and floral designers.

## Features

- ✅ **Hero Section** - Stunning full-screen hero with call-to-action
- ✅ **Services Showcase** - Display your event planning and floral services
- ✅ **Portfolio Gallery** - Showcase your best events with hover effects
- ✅ **Testimonials** - Client reviews with star ratings
- ✅ **Why Choose Us** - Highlight your unique value propositions
- ✅ **Newsletter Signup** - Email subscription form
- ✅ **Contact Form** - Easy-to-use contact form
- ✅ **Responsive Design** - Mobile-first, works on all devices
- ✅ **SEO Optimized** - Structured data and meta tags included
- ✅ **Modern Design** - Elegant typography and smooth animations

## Color Scheme

- **Primary Color**: Red (#dc2626) - Perfect for events and celebrations
- **Accent Color**: Green (#22c55e) - Fresh and vibrant
- **Gold Highlights**: (#fbbf24) - Elegant and luxurious
- **Neutral Grays**: Professional and clean

## Typography

- **Headings**: Playfair Display (serif) - Elegant and sophisticated
- **Body**: Inter (sans-serif) - Modern and readable

## Sections

1. **Hero** - Full-screen introduction with background image
2. **Services** - Four main service offerings with icons
3. **Why Choose Us** - Three key differentiators with icons
4. **Portfolio** - Three featured events with hover overlays
5. **Testimonials** - Client reviews with ratings
6. **Newsletter** - Email subscription form
7. **Contact** - Contact form section
8. **Footer** - Company info, links, and contact details

## Customization Guide

### 1. Update Content
- Replace placeholder text with your actual content
- Update service descriptions
- Add your real portfolio images
- Replace testimonials with real client reviews

### 2. Update Images
- Hero background image
- Portfolio images
- Testimonial author photos
- Why Choose Us section image

### 3. Update Contact Information
- Phone number
- Email address
- Physical address
- Business hours

### 4. Update Colors (Optional)
Edit `css/variables.css` to change the color scheme:
- Primary colors (red tones)
- Accent colors (green tones)
- Gold highlights

### 5. Add Your Logo
Replace the text logo in the navigation with your actual logo image.

## Form Integration

The contact form and newsletter form are currently set up for client-side validation. To make them functional:

1. **Backend Integration**: Connect to your email service (SendGrid, Mailchimp, etc.)
2. **API Endpoint**: Create an API endpoint to handle form submissions
3. **Email Service**: Use services like Resend, SendGrid, or Mailgun
