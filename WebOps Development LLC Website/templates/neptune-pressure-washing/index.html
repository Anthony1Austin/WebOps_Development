Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our pressure washing services

How often should I pressure wash my house?

We recommend pressure washing your home's exterior every 1-2 years to maintain its appearance and protect your investment.

Do you use eco-friendly cleaning solutions?

Yes, we use safe, eco-friendly cleaning solutions that are safe for your family, pets, and plants.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed and insured for your protection and peace of mind.

How long does a typical pressure washing job take?

Most residential jobs take 2-4 hours depending on the size of your property and the services requested.

Do I need to be home during the service?

No, you don't need to be home. We can work around your schedule and will contact you when the job is complete.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Massillon, Canton, North Canton, and surrounding areas in Stark and Summit counties.
