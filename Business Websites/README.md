# Business Website Template

A professional corporate website template perfect for businesses, agencies, and organizations. Built on the Basic Website Structure with business-focused features.

## Business-Specific Features

- ✅ **Services Showcase** - Highlight your offerings
- ✅ **Team Section** - Introduce your team members
- ✅ **Testimonials** - Build trust with client reviews
- ✅ **Case Studies** - Show your work and results
- ✅ **FAQ Section** - Answer common questions
- ✅ **Contact Forms** - Multiple contact options
- ✅ **Company Timeline** - Show your history
- ✅ **Client Logos** - Display partnerships

## Customization Guide

### 1. Essential Sections
- **About Us** - Company history, mission, values
- **Services** - Detailed service offerings
- **Team** - Team member profiles with photos
- **Testimonials** - Client reviews and ratings
- **Case Studies/Portfolio** - Success stories
- **FAQ** - Common questions and answers
- **Contact** - Multiple contact methods

### 2. Business Schema Markup
Add Organization schema with:
- Business name and logo
- Contact information
- Social media profiles
- Business hours
- Address and location

### 3. Trust Building Elements
- Client testimonials with photos
- Client logo carousel
- Awards and certifications
- Statistics/metrics section
- Security badges (if applicable)

### 4. Lead Generation
- Multiple CTA buttons
- Contact forms
- Newsletter signup
- Free consultation booking
- Downloadable resources

## Recommended Additions

### Sections to Consider
- **Pricing** - If you offer packages
- **Blog/News** - Industry insights
- **Resources** - Whitepapers, guides
- **Careers** - Job listings
- **Partners** - Business partnerships

- CRM integration (HubSpot, Salesforce)
